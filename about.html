About Us

Phi Beta Lambda

Organization Structure

Five committees focus on different fields and intersections of business and pursue engaging and challenging curriculums to foster well-rounded members. The committees include consulting, finance, marketing, technology, and biotech.

In addition, PBL offers its consulting services to a wide range of clients from budding start-ups to Fortune 500 firms through CT Consulting and its client teams.

Our Values

Leaders Who Care

We understand the value of giving back to the PBL community, each offering our time, expertise, and care to shape the PBL experience.

Teamwork

We thrive on the diversity of our members, coming together to openly share ideas while working as one team.

Leave Your Legacy

We provide and value opportunities for members to find new ways to make lasting impacts to our community.

Community First

We understand the stress of college life and come together to share the #phibetaluv from saving each other seats at the library to traveling abroad together.

Our Community

With an intimate organizational structure and a semester filled with exciting social activities and outings, PBL's community is a family of diverse personalities and passions; through a 1-on-1 mentorship program and social events planned by our Internal Networking committee

Our Alumni

Our members and alumni, backed by their PBL experiences, pursue careers with industry leaders in a diverse array of fields from management consulting and investment banking to software engineering and product management.

Our Partners

Boston Consulting Group is a global consulting firm that partners with leaders in business and society to tackle their most important challenges and capture their greatest opportunities. Our success depends on a spirit of deep collaboration and a global community of diverse individuals determined to make the world and each other better every day.

At PwC, our purpose is to build trust in society and solve important problems. In an increasingly complex world, we help intricate systems function, adapt and evolve so they can deliver sustained outcomes for communities and society – whether they are capital markets, tax systems or the economic systems within which business and society exist.

At EY, our purpose is Building a better working world. The insights and quality services we provide help build trust and confidence in the capital markets and in economies the world over. We develop outstanding leaders who team to deliver on our promises to all our stakeholders. In so doing, we play a critical role in building a better working world for our people, for our clients and for our communities.

NVIDIA is a global leader in accelerated computing, driving innovation in artificial intelligence, gaming, and high-performance computing. Our mission is to solve the world’s toughest challenges through cutting-edge technology, empowering industries to push boundaries and deliver transformative outcomes. With a focus on collaboration and sustainability, we strive to build solutions that advance humanity and improve lives, from revolutionizing healthcare to enabling the future of autonomous vehicles and AI-powered systems.

Become a Sponsor
