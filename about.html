About Us

Future Business Leaders of America - Phi Beta Lambda

Organization Structure

Six committees focus on different fields and intersections of business and pursue engaging and challenging curriculums to foster well-rounded members. The six committees include consulting, finance, marketing, technology, media design, and bio-tech.

In addition, PBL offers its consulting services to a wide range of clients from budding start-ups to Fortune 500 firms through CT Consulting and its client teams.

Our Values

Leaders Who Care

We understand the value of giving back to the PBL community, each offering our time, expertise, and care to shape the PBL experience.

Teamwork

We thrive on the diversity of our members, coming together to openly share ideas while working as one team.

Leave Your Legacy

We provide and value opportunities for members to find new ways to make lasting impacts to our community.

Community First

We understand the stress of college life and come together to share the #phibetaluv from saving each other seats at the library to traveling abroad together.

Our Community

With an intimate organizational structure and a semester filled with exciting social activities and outings, PBL's community is a family of diverse personalities and passions; through a 1-on-1 mentorship program and social events planned by our Internal Networking committee

Our Alumni

Our members and alumni, backed by their PBL experiences, pursue careers with industry leaders in a diverse array of fields from management consulting and investment banking to software engineering and product management.

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this web site.
