Infosession 1

@ 8-10pm Barrows 166

Come meet our members and learn about the amazing opportunities and community PBL has to offer! You'll have the chance to hear more about our industry-specific committees and project teams, our favorite PBL experiences, and the vast professional and personal resources available to our members.

Infosession 2

@ 8-10pm Barrows 126

Mirroring Infosession #1, this event is another opportunity to come meet our members and learn about the amazing opportunities and community PBL has to offer! While we welcome interested applicants to mingle with our officers in any capacity, you are not required to attend both infosessions.

Social Mixer

@ 7-9pm Stephens Lounge

Head out to MLK's Stephens Lounge for the perfect opportunity to meet and get to know our members on a more personal level before submitting your application. Whether you have a burning question about a particular committee or just want to leave a lasting impression before interviews, our Social Mixer is the perfect chance to talk about your personal or professional passions and gauge if PBL is right for you!

Application Due
